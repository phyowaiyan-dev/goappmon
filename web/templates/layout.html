About this software

Built for control, speed, and clean release management

GoAppMon is a lightweight application control center for mobile and web products. It gives teams a fast admin surface for version policy, maintenance messaging, banners, and feature flags.

This product is powered by Phyo Wai Yan, who focuses on production-grade software, AI automation, and scalable digital products.

Why it matters

Ship safer app updates, keep users informed, and manage your rollout strategy from one simple dashboard.

What it supports

Initial setup, secure admin login, public status APIs, and feature flag management in a single Go binary.
